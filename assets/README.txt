Place your branding here:
- favicon.jpg (64x64 or 32x32). Referenced by /index.html link[rel="icon"].
- header logo.jpg (at least 256x256). Referenced in header and footer.

Temporary fallbacks are auto-generated in the browser if these files are missing (script draws a simple "F" badge), but replacing them with your actual images is recommended.

- Add images into assets/nfts to replace the placeholder gallery sources in assets/script.js.
